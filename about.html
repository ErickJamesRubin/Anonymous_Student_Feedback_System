About Our System

The Anonymous Student Feedback System at Northern Bukidnon State College (NBSC) is designed to empower every student voice while maintaining complete anonymity. Our platform provides a safe, secure, and accessible way for students to share their honest feedback, concerns, and suggestions about their academic experience.



Our Mission

To create a transparent and responsive educational environment where every student feels heard, valued, and confident that their feedback will drive meaningful change in our institution.
