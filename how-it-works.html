How It Works

A simple and secure process to make your voice heard anonymously.

System Active
Process Overview
01
Create Your Feedback
Visit the Submit Feedback page and share your thoughts, concerns, or suggestions. Choose your feedback category — Academic, Facilities, Student Services, or Administration — and provide detailed information to help us understand your perspective.
02
Stay Anonymous
Your identity is completely protected. We don't collect any personal information, IP addresses, or identifiable data. You have the option to submit publicly — visible to other students — or anonymously, visible only to administrators.
03
Review Process
Our dedicated team reviews all feedback submissions promptly. Each submission is carefully evaluated for validity and importance. Urgent matters are prioritized and addressed immediately.
04
Action & Response
Relevant departments are notified and work on addressing your feedback. You can track the status in the Feedback Hub — Under Review, Planned, Acknowledged, or Resolved. The community can see updates and progress on public submissions.
05
Continuous Improvement
Your feedback drives real change. We analyze patterns and trends to improve policies, services, and facilities. Regular reports are shared with the NBSC community showing how student feedback has made a difference.
🔐
Your Anonymity is Guaranteed
No names, student IDs, or personal data are collected or stored with your submission.
⚡
Fast Review Process
All submissions are reviewed by the admin team within 3–5 business days.
📣
Your Voice Matters
Feedback directly influences school policies, teaching practices, and campus improvements.
